About

Michael enjoys spending time in nature going on hikes with friends. He really enjoys running and is known to be almost faster than Usain Bolt. He works-out quite often; the next Ronnie Coleman, the kids call him.  

Michael also plays many musical instruments which are cello, piano, euphonium, trombone, tuba,  and trumpet. In his spare time he likes to kick back with the boys and play video games.
